Artistry and NU Sci Magazines

Editorial Design

This past year, I worked on two campus magazine design teams, Artistry Magazine and NU Sci Magazine. This was my first time designing magazine spreads, so I was initially unsure of my skills, but I quickly picked up the skills necessary to design unique, readable pages.

Pictured to the left are some of the articles I designed throughout the semester for the two magazines. One of the most interesting articles to design was the X Ambassadors article, for which I created a vector portrait of all the band members. I then incorporated colors from the concert photos to tie the spread together. Overall, working on these two magazines helped to expand my design knowledge and experience in working as part of a design team.
